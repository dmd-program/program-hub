Are you a student enrolled in the program? Slack is a great way to get your questions answered quickly by the program community. Visit the Slack group here: dmd-program.slack.com

The Penn State DMD Club is the official student-run organization of the Digital Multimedia Design program. Become a member if you would like to help foster a growing community of interdisciplinary digital artist and design students. It's a great place to network with your peers, industry professionals, program alumni: Penn State DMD Club on Facebook.

Don't miss another program update. Sign up today for the DMD Listserv by sending an email to L-DIGMD-subscribe-request@lists.psu.edu. If you are already signed up, but would like to unsubscribe, send an email to L-DIGMD-unsubscribe-request@lists.psu.edu.
